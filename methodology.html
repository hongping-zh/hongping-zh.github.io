Methodology

Methodology Notes

The study measures end-to-end LLM inference energy for FP16, NF4, and INT8, focusing on relative energy change against FP16.

Relative energy change

ΔE% = (E_quantized - E_FP16) / E_FP16 × 100

Positive values indicate quantization consumes more energy than FP16; negative values indicate savings.

Power measurement

Energy measurements use NVIDIA Management Library power sampling, idle-power baselines, warm-up runs, repeated inference measurements, coefficient-of-variation checks, and within-platform comparisons against FP16 baselines.

Empirical calibration

ΔE%(N)=a+b/N

This model is a compact empirical descriptor of the measured sign-reversal pattern, not a universal scaling law.

What the method supports

  • within-platform relative energy comparison
  • empirical sign-reversal detection
  • operational crossover brackets
  • descriptive calibration for measured records
