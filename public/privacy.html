Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the LLM Energy Benchmark website (hongping-zh.github.io) handles visitor data. This is a static, open-data research website hosted on GitHub Pages. It has no accounts, no login, and no server-side backend.

1. What We Collect

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand aggregate, anonymous traffic (page views, approximate region, device type, referrer). IP anonymization (anonymize_ip) is enabled, so your full IP address is never stored by us.

We do NOT collect:

  • Names, emails, or any personally identifying account data
  • Form submissions (the site has no data-entry forms)
  • Payment information

2. Cookies

Google Analytics sets first-party cookies (e.g. _ga) to distinguish unique visitors and measure sessions. You can block these via your browser settings or a content blocker without affecting site functionality.

3. Third-Party Services

To render pages, the site loads assets from third parties whose own privacy policies apply: Google Fonts, Tailwind CDN, and Google Analytics / Tag Manager. No benchmark data you view is tied to your identity.

4. Data Retention

We retain no personal data ourselves. Aggregated, anonymized analytics are retained by Google according to the GA4 default retention period.

5. Open Source & Data

All benchmark data, scripts, and the site source are public: github.com/hongping-zh/ecocompute-ai.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

Questions? Open an issue at github.com/hongping-zh/ecocompute-ai/issues or reach out to Hongping Zhang on GitHub.
